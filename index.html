An Interactive Map of U.S. History

Mohammed Al-Ali, 79-240 Final Project, Summer 2026

This map tells the story of American history through place. Each event sits on the state where it happened, grounded in the readings from our course and tied to the theme of contested freedom. Click a state in bold to see its events, or filter by period below.

Note on borders: the map uses present-day state outlines, but borders changed over time, so each event names the actual jurisdiction when it differs. For example, John Brown's 1859 raid is on Virginia, because Harpers Ferry only became part of West Virginia in 1863.
